# Support

Thanks for using Flama! Here is where to go depending on what you need.

## Documentation

The fastest answer is usually in the docs: [https://flama.dev/docs/](https://flama.dev/docs/).
Start with the [quickstart](https://flama.dev/docs/getting-started/quickstart/) and the
[CLI guide](https://flama.dev/docs/flama-cli/serve/).

## Questions and ideas

For "how do I...?" questions, usage help, and feature ideas, open a thread in
[GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/vortico/flama/discussions). It is the best place
to reach the maintainers and the community.

## Bugs

If you have found a bug, please open an
[issue](https://github.com/vortico/flama/issues/new/choose) using the bug report template,
and include a minimal reproducible example, the Flama version, and your environment.

## Security

To report a vulnerability, follow the process in
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Do not open a public issue for security problems.

## Commercial support

For commercial support, consulting, or to hire the team behind Flama, visit
[vortico.tech/hire](https://vortico.tech/hire/).
